Adreen's Journal App

Welcome to your personal journal app! Here you can add, view, and manage your journal entries. Click "Add New Entry" to get started, or use the search bar to find specific entries. Enjoy writing!

As Humans we have a natural tendency to write down our thoughts and experiences. This journal app is designed to help you capture those moments, reflect on your day, and keep a record of your life.

Note: Your entries are stored locally in your browser, so they will not be accessible from other devices or browsers. Make sure to back up your entries if you want to keep them safe. You can do this by exporting your entries to a file or copying them to another location.

Privacy: Your journal entries are private and not shared with anyone. This app does not collect any personal data or share your entries with third parties. You can write freely without worrying about privacy concerns.

Features:

  • Add new journal entries with a date and content.
  • View all your entries in a list.
  • Search for specific entries by keywords.
  • Dark mode for comfortable reading at night.
  • Simple and user-friendly interface.
  • Option to cancel or save entries.
